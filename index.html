About Our Charity

MAKING A BETTER TOMORROW

Raising The Communities (RTC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable communities in Uganda. We focus on health, education, poverty alleviation, and environmental conservation.

Our mission is to empower individuals through capacity building, advocacy, and sustainable initiatives, enabling them to lead self-sustained, dignified lives. Founded by poverty activists, RTC is committed to creating lasting change by working with local communities and partners to address the root causes of poverty and build a brighter future.
