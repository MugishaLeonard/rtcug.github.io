About Our Charity

MAKING A BETTER TOMORROW

Welcome to Raising The Communities (RTC), a dedicated non-profit organization committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable communities in Uganda. Established by passionate poverty activists, RTC aims to address critical issues affecting our communities through a comprehensive approach that integrates health, education, poverty alleviation, and environmental conservation.

MISSION

Our Mission is to strengthen the capacity of vulnerable communities to improve their standards of living through comprehensive capacity building, evidence-based interventions, advocacy, and strategic partnerships.

VISION

To foster better, self-sustained, and developed communities where individuals thrive and contribute to a sustainable future.

Goals

  1. 1. To improve the health standards of people in Uganda.
  2. 2. To alleviate poverty, food insecurity, and improve the standards of living among people in Uganda.
  3. 3. To raise literacy levels among children and the youth in Uganda.
  4. 4. To promote proper utilization of natural resources and conservation of the environment.

Objectives

  1. 1. To promote health and utilization of health services among the vulnerable populations through increasing awareness and community engagement.
  2. 2. To reduce poverty levels and improve living conditions among the vulnerable communities in Uganda.
  3. 3. To increase literacy levels among the vulnerable children and youth through supporting family and community-based efforts.
  4. 4. To promote community participation in the sustainable use, management, and protection of natural resources.
